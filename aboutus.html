About us

Floyem Pharmaceuticals & Chemicals Limited is an indigenous company that specializes in the distribution and marketing of raw materials to both Pharmaceutical Companies and Food Industries.
Our efficient global logistics system and supply chain helps us deliver high-quality, pure, and natural extracts promptly without interruption.  We also promote local agriculture by supporting farmers in Nigeria to export high quality raw materials and food products to global markets.   With Floyem, say goodbye to counterfeit or substandard pharmaceutical products, regulatory hurdles, inefficient logistics, late distribution, and increased production cost.

Our History

30 YEARS OF PHARMACEUTICAL EXCELLENCE AND FOOD SAFETY

Since its inception in 1993, Floyem Pharmaceutical and Chemical Industries Ltd has helped numerous industries in Texas, U.S.A. and the diaspora.
We thought of how best to save the world and realized that a better world starts from better nutrition which is tied to better food industries. 
By establishing a local presence in Nigeria, we eliminated the hurdles and complications of importation and deliver high-quality pharmaceutical solutions right where you need them. At Floyem, we connect local industries with upscale pharmacy products and services abroad and also export Nigeria’s finest raw materials to the world.
We are here to help food and pharmaceutical industries save cost, provide healthy products to their customers and increase their company reputation by providing high quality and healthy raw materials at cost-effective prices.
Over the years, we have expanded to establishing offices in the US, China, Lagos, Kano, and Akure. Shaped by our history, we understand that trust, reliability, consistency and experience are essential to being a reliable supplier.
